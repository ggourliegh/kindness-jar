## ğŸ“ **QR Code Specifications**

### **For Print:**
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Size:** At least 2cm x 2cm (0.8" x 0.8")
- **Format:** PNG or SVG (vector)
- **Colors:** High contrast (black on white recommended)
- **Error Correction:** Level H (30% - allows for logos/damage)

### **For Digital:**
- **Resolution:** 72-150 DPI
- **Size:** 200x200px minimum
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **File size:** Under 100KB for web

## ğŸ“± **Testing Your QR Code**

Before printing or sharing:

1. **Test on multiple devices:**
   - iPhone camera app
   - Android camera app
   - QR code scanner apps

2. **Check from different distances:**
   - Should scan from 6-12 inches away
   - Larger codes scan from further away

3. **Verify the URL:**
   - Make sure it goes to the correct page
   - Check that the page loads properly

4. **Test in different lighting:**
   - Bright light
   - Low light
   - Ensure good contrast
