## ğŸ“‹ **Design Specifications**

### **For Printing:**
- **Size:** 8.5 x 11 inches (standard letter)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **QR Code Size:** 4 x 4 inches minimum
- **Margins:** 0.5 inches on all sides
- **File Format:** PDF (best for printing)

### **Colors to Use:**
- **Background:** White or light cream (#FFF9F5)
- **Title:** Navy blue (#2D3748)
- **Accents:** Soft pink (#FFB6C1), light blue (#87CEEB)
- **QR Code:** Black and white (don't change!)

### **Fonts:**
- **Title:** Playfair Display, Montserrat, or Poppins (Bold)
- **Body:** Open Sans, Roboto, or Lato (Regular)

## ğŸ’¡ **Pro Tips**

1. **Test Before Mass Printing:**
   - Print one copy first
   - Test QR code scanning
   - Check colors and readability

2. **QR Code Placement:**
   - Center of poster
   - At least 1 inch from edges
   - Large enough to scan from 2 feet away

3. **Keep It Simple:**
   - Don't overcrowd the design
   - QR code needs breathing room
   - Clear, readable text

4. **Multiple Sizes:**
   - Create 8.5 x 11 for flyers
   - Create 18 x 24 for posters
   - Create 3.5 x 2 for business cards
